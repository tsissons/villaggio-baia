Suite #5

Suite #5 is a lovely one bedroom suite located on the first floor of the building, overlooking the pool and recrational grounds. This suite had an unbeatable extremely large terrace which is great for entertaining, enjoing evening meals and relaxing whilst the sun sets behind the trees in the gorgeous Calabrian evenings.

Suite #22

Suite #22 is a one-bedroom suite situated on the top floor, with a peek-a-boo view of the the ocean and craggy mountain range. This suite offers a bit more privacy than Suite #5, and has a fair-sized terrace, as well as a wrap around terrace off the bedroom. There is a fully equipped kitchen and a king-sized bed, all coming together to make this suite perfect for a peaceful romantic retreat in the heart of Calabria.
